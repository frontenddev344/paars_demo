Institutional strengthening in governance and accountability

A strong regulatory system depends on clear roles, effective oversight, and accountability. We help governments define institutional responsibilities, improve coordination, and reinforce accountability.

  • Assessing and optimizing the institutional setup for Better Regulation, ensuring clear mandates and responsibilities.

  • Designing or refining regulatory oversight bodies, improving transparency and coherence in rulemaking.

  • Developing accountability frameworks, including independent reviews, public reporting mechanisms, and performance evaluations.

A Better Regulation framework should provide a structured approach to policymaking, ensuring that regulations are necessary, effective, and aligned with national priorities. PAARS supports governments in designing and improving regulatory policies by:

  • Assessing existing regulatory frameworks to identify gaps, inefficiencies, and areas for improvement.

  • Aligning regulatory policies with international best practices, including OECD principles for Better Regulation.

  • Developing customized policy recommendations to help governments enhance regulatory quality and coherence.

Regulatory Policy Design and Optimization

Regulatory Tools & Implementation

Regulatory frameworks must be supported by the right tools to ensure effective policymaking. PAARS helps governments design, refine, and implement key Better Regulation tools, including:

  • Stakeholder engagement strategies and public consultation mechanisms that improve transparency and inclusivity.

  • Regulatory Impact Assessments, both ex ante and ex post, to ensure that policies are evidence-based and effective.

  • Guidelines, procedural manuals, and regulatory roadmaps to standardize and improve the policymaking process.

Regulation should support, not burden, businesses and citizens. PAARS works with governments to evaluate administrative burdens and streamline regulatory processes to improve efficiency and compliance. We assist with:

  • Mapping and analyzing administrative procedures to identify unnecessary complexity.

  • Developing simplification strategies, such as one-stop-shops, digitalization of services, and risk-based approaches to regulation.

  • Assessing the impact of administrative burdens on businesses, including small-medium sized enterprises (SMEs), and citizens to ensure proportional and efficient regulation.

Regulatory Innovation & Digital Transformation

The regulatory environment is constantly evolving, and governments need innovative approaches to keep pace with technological advancements, digital economies, and emerging industries. PAARS helps governments modernize regulation by:

  • Integrating digital tools into the regulatory process, including e-consultation platforms and AI-driven regulatory impact assessments.

  • Advising on regulatory sandboxes, allowing governments to test new policies in controlled environments.

  • Developing future-proof regulatory frameworks that can adapt to technological change and emerging policy challenges.
