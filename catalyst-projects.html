What We Offer

Our Services Under Catalyst Projects

Regulatory reform goes beyond drafting good policies: It requires sound execution, inclusive engagement, and strong enforcement. Through Catalyst Projects, PAARS provides tailored consulting services to help governments design and implement reforms that are practical, strategic, and results-driven.

Stakeholder engagement & public consultation design

Well-informed regulation depends on effective and meaningful consultation. We support the design and execution of public consultations that are inclusive, data-driven, and context-sensitive.

  • Developing consultation strategies tailored to the policy and
    institutional context

  • Identifying and engaging key stakeholders
    across sectors

  • Designing digital and hybrid consultation tools, including AI-
    assisted interfaces

  • Designing consultation questions and documents to improve
    clarity and accessibility

  • Analyzing responses and producing insights that inform
    decision-making

Clear, coherent legal and policy instruments are essential for implementation and compliance. PAARS provides expert support in drafting, reviewing, and improving regulatory proposals.

  • Drafting new legal and policy instruments aligned with
    international standards

  • Reviewing existing regulations to improve clarity and
    eliminate inconsistencies

  • Ensuring legal coherence across institutions
    and sectors

  • Developing guidelines and manuals to support application
    and compliance

Regulatory proposals & legal drafting

Regulatory delivery & implementation

Effective regulation depends on clear responsibilities, institutional coordination, and realistic enforcement. We support governments in planning and executing implementation strategies that make regulation work in practice.

  • Developing implementation plans and
    roadmaps

  • Coordinating delivery across ministries, agencies, and
    oversight bodies

  • Providing technical assistance to executing
    teams

  • Advising on enforcement strategies that promote fairness
    and compliance

  • Identifying gaps and proposing corrective actions during
    rollout

To ensure long-term effectiveness, regulation must be tracked, evaluated, and adapted. We help governments monitor performance and make data-driven improvements over time.

  • Defining performance indicators and
    tracking frameworks

  • Designing evaluation methodologies and
    feedback loops

  • Assessing regulatory outcomes across
    sectors

  • Identifying implementation gaps or unintended
    effects

  • Recommending adjustments to keep policies fit
    for purpose

Monitoring & supervision

Regulatory consultation support for private sector & civil society

Participating in regulatory consultations can be complex. We help businesses, SMEs, professional and industry associations and civil society actors engage effectively and constructively in policy processes.

  • Analyzing draft regulations to assess risks
    and opportunities

  • Reviewing proposals and preparing written
    submissions

  • Advising on engagement strategies
    and formats

  • Supporting coordination across internal and
    external stakeholders

  • Facilitating communication with
    regulators
